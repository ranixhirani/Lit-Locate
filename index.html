About

A library is a place where you can find and use books, movies, music, and other materials. It can be a physical building or a website. Libraries have librarians who can help you find information. They also provide quiet areas for studying and sometimes have computers and internet access. Libraries can be for the public, schools, companies, or individuals. They offer different services depending on the type of library. Some libraries also host programs and activities for learning. Modern libraries can be accessed online, allowing you to borrow materials from home. The services provided by libraries are often called library services or information services.
